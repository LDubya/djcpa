About David Jonathan B.S., M. Ed.

Dedicated to advocating for children and parents, "DJ" has over 35 years working with children, adult students, and parents to ensure that the goals expressed on students' IEPs, EPs and other documents of support are implemented. DJ was Executive Producer of a Florida County's longest running, locally produced (by kids) TV and AM radio programs. He has a heart, after God, to see the success of children with unique abilities. He has a Bachelor of Science in Physical Education and a Masters in Behavioral Disorders. DJ has many awards such as, but not limited to, nominated for Presidential Point of Light, publishing awards, local and state teacher nominations, recognition by the Florida State Senate, special day(s) of honor by city mayors, just to name a few.
